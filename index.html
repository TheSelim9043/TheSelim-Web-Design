CSS3 Styling

Cascading Style Sheets (Cascading Style Templates or Cascading Format Sheets, known as CSS ) is a markup language that offers extra possibilities in the field of text and formatting in addition to HTML . It allows you to create generic templates for Internet pages, or it can be used to independently change the style of letters, that is, their properties such as color, font, size. The most important feature of this technique is its flexibility in use.

Graphic Design

Graphic design is a creative process that involves organizing text and images in a perceptible and visible plane, two-dimensional or three-dimensional, to convey a message, develop a visual or visualize a thought. It can be applied digitally or non-digitally in many environments such as printing, screen, motion film, animation, interior design, packaging design.
